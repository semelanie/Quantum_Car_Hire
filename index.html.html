FAQ

A few more questions

What do I need to rent a car?

A valid driver's license held for at least one year, a passport or Seychellois ID, and a refundable deposit at pickup.

Is there a minimum age to rent?

18 years or older, with a valid driver's license held for at least one year.

Can I add a baby car seat?

Yes, on any vehicle — tick the option when booking or mention it when you message us.

Where can I pick up and drop off?

Airport, jetty, and hotel are our most common points, but we're not limited to these — we'll arrange any address on Mahé at booking.

What's the fuel policy?

Handed over full, returned full. Nearest fuel stations are listed in your confirmation.

Is insurance included?

Third-party cover comes standard with every rental. A full-cover upgrade is available if you'd like extra peace of mind.

Can I extend my rental?

Usually yes, subject to availability. Message us on WhatsApp before your return time and we'll do our best to accommodate it.

What if I have a breakdown or accident?

Call or WhatsApp us right away at +248 2599 333. We're reachable 24/7 and will guide you through next steps.

Is there a cleaning fee?

Normal wear isn't charged. A fee applies only if a car comes back excessively dirty.

When is my deposit returned?

The same day you return the car, condition permitting.

Can I get a receipt?

Yes — just ask and we'll email one over after your rental ends.

What if I return the car late?

A late fee may apply. If you know you'll be delayed, message us ahead of time and we'll work with you on it.
