FAQ

A few more questions

Is there a minimum age to rent?

21 years old minimum, 23+ for the Jimny and Ertiga.

What's the fuel policy?

Handed over full, returned full. Nearest fuel stations are listed in your confirmation.

Can I add a baby car seat?

Yes, on any vehicle — tick the option when booking or mention it when you call.

Is there a cleaning fee?

Normal wear isn't charged. A fee applies only if a car comes back excessively dirty.
